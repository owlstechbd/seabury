True Supply Chain Finance Program

Seabury TFX utilize a unique multi-stage funding approach to enhance the efficiency of supply chain finance that can streamline your operations and optimize cash flow.

Stage 1
Pre-Shipping Financing

Access to funds for suppliers before goods are shipped, ensuring they can manage production costs effectively.

Stage 2
In-Transit Financing

Financing options available while goods are in transit, providing liquidity during this critical period.

Stage 3
Post-Shipping Financing

Funds can be accessed upon delivery and invoice approval, allowing for immediate cash flow after sales.

This structured approach reduces financial strain on suppliers and enhances cash flow across the supply chain, ensuring smoother operations and improved buyer-supplier relationships.

About Us

Seabury TFX

Seabury TFX (“STFX”) is a Hong Kong and U.S. based company providing trade finance solutions to supply chains around the world currently servicing companies in 35+ countries. STFX’s unique platform harnesses the power of data and technology to originate trade finance transactions. STFX’s innovative trade finance solutions utilize supply chain data and other supplementary data sources to facilitate onboarding, origination and servicing.

Seabury TFX is recognized as a disruptor in the trade finance space, voted "Best Supplier Finance Provider" in 2016. Our commitment to providing uncorrelated returns with low volatility makes us an attractive choice for investors seeking stable and profitable opportunities.

Seabury Capital Group

Seabury Capital Group LLC (“Seabury Capital”) operates a number of specialty finance, investment and merchant banking, technology, and software companies with a core focus anchored in aviation, aerospace & defense, and financial services & technology. Since its founding in 1995, Seabury Capital has historically been the investment holding company of its founder, John E. Luth, operating as a venture capital firm with ownership stakes in software and asset management businesses servicing the aviation and travel industries.

Seabury Capital ended the year 2021 with over $1.3 billion in assets and intends to leverage those resources to launch one or more specialized investment funds in aviation, travel, and technology in 2022, under a newly incorporated investment fund manager, Seabury Capital Management LLC.
